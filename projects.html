The Virtual Herbal Garden is an interactive digital platform showcasing medicinal
plants used in AYUSH practices (Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy).
It features 3D plant models, comprehensive information on botanical and medicinal properties, multimedia content, advanced search tools, and guided virtual tours. Designed for students,
it provides an immersive and educational experience, promoting awareness of traditional medicine through modern technology.
